# THE APHRODI ARCHIVE

> From Eindhoven to Immortality

A cinematic football biography website telling the complete story of **Aphrodi**, from a forgotten youngster in Eindhoven to a legendary No.9 who conquered Europe and the world.

---

## Overview

The Aphrodi Archive is a storytelling website designed as a digital book.

Instead of presenting statistics alone, the project focuses on narrative, atmosphere, emotion, and legacy.

The story follows Aphrodi through every major stage of his career:

* PSV Eindhoven
* Strasbourg
* Eintracht Frankfurt
* Valencia
* Arsenal
* Atlético Madrid
* Italy National Team
* FC Barcelona
* Juventus

The website combines:

* Long-form storytelling
* Cinematic visual design
* Interactive chapter navigation
* Animated transitions
* Legacy posters
* Career statistics
* Quotes and personal reflections

## Design Philosophy

This project was inspired by:

* Football autobiographies
* Netflix sports documentaries
* Modern editorial websites
* Luxury magazine layouts
* Cinematic storytelling

The goal was simple:

> Not to tell the story of a football player.
>
> But to tell the story of a man who refused to surrender to fate.
